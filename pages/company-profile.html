ANGU Safe Manufacturing Co., Ltd., the best safe OEM/ODM expert in South China. The history of the Co. can be traced back to 1984, and in the year of 2013, in order to expand the business, new Angu Safe Manufacturing Co.,Ltd was established.

In the year of 2013, in order to expand business from oversea, our Hong kong office was set up with HK Angu Safe Co.,LIMITED established.

ANGU Safe Manufacturing Co., Ltd., is a high-tech comprehensive innovative enterprise specialized in R&D, manufacturing and marketing of safes. Our factory is located in Shunde, Foshan of China, the soonest developing area in the center of Pearl River Delta in southern China, which is also near to Canton and Hongkong.

Our 10,000m2 production base equipped with advanced production facilities and hundreds of proficient workers, theses ensuring all orders will be delivered on time. Our excellent quality and outstanding service, makes us become the main safes supplier of some famous safe brand names. 

We will comply with the highest international manufacturing standards, provide high quality products with best service to customers from all over the world. Any trade enquiry or factory visiting will be warmly welcome.
