1, To open the door for the first time, please use the outer battery box and follow the steps below. After opening the door, please fit the batteries in the inner battery box and take out the outer battery box for normal operations.

2, To open the door:

Press “#” to awaken the system, Input your code (factory setting code is 1234), and confirm by pressing “#”, “OPEN” displays, now turn the key right to the end, then turn the handle to open the door within 5 seconds.

3, To change the code:

Press the button behind the door cover, “----”displays, input your new 1 to 8 digits code, and confirm by pressing “#”, “INTO” displays, as indicates the code has been successfully changed.

4, Press “#”, then press “*” to hide the code before entering the code, after your open the door, the code will turn back to display.

5, Press the key “*” to delete the wrong input.

6, In case you forget the code, you can open the door by using the override key instead of inputting the code. After opening the door, please follow step 3 to change your code.

7. Low battery warning

When “Lo-BATTE” displays as indicates low battery, please change the batteries as soon as possible. You can open the door by using the outer battery box when low battery.
