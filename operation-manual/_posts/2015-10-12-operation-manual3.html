1. To open the door for the first time.

Please open the door by the override key for the first time.

Please note: please add the super management fingerprint and management fingerprint immediately after opening the door.

2. To add management fingerprint:

a) To add super management fingerprint:

Press the green button behind the door cover, the red lamp on the window lights, put the super management fingerprint on the window, “OK” displays as indicates the fingerprint has been successfully added. If “NO” displays,that indicates the fingerprint fails to be added.

b) To add management fingerprint:

Press the green button behind the door cover, put the super management fingerprint on the window for authorization. “OK” displays as indicates the authoriation is successful. Then put management fingerprint on the window, “OK” displays as indicates the management fingerprint has been successfully added.

Please note: the lock can only reserve 3 fingerprints, one super management fingerprint, one management fingerprint, one user fingerprint.

3. To lock the door by user fingerprint:

Close the door, press the button on the panel, the red lamp on the window lights, put the user fingerprint on the window, “OK” displays, the door will be locked automatically. And the fingerprint is successfully added.

4. To open the door by user fingerprint:

Press the button on the panel, the red lamp on the window lights, put the user fingerprint on the window, “OK” displays, the door will be opened automatically, at the same time, the the original fingerprint will be deleted.

5. Quickly lock: keep pressing the button on the panel for 2 seconds, the door will be locked automatically.

6. Three times wrong try will starts a 15-minute freezing period.

7. To open the door by super management fingerprint or management fingerprint:

Press the button on the panel, the red lamp lights, put the super management fingerprint or management fingerprint on the window, “OK” displays, the door will be opened automatically. In this case, the user fingerprint will be deleted.

8. To delete all the fingerprint:

Keep pressing the red button on the door cover, at the same time press the button on the panel till “OK” displays as indicate all the fingerprint has been successfully deleted.

9. In case the door can’t be opened by the fingerprint, you can open the door by using the override key instead of the fingerprint. After opening the door, please use a new fingerprint to lock the door, now you can operate the safe as usual.

10.Low battery warning

After the door is opened, “di-dii-di” sounds as indicate low battery, please change the batteries as soon as posible. You can open the door by using the outer battery box when low battery.
